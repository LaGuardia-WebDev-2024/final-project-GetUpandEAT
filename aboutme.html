About Me

I love all creative and artistic endeavors but I'd like to pursue writing most of all. I prefer writing essays and plays but will sometimes dabbles in poetry (when I'm feeling extra angsty). I've studied playwriting at Roundabout Theater Company, expository writing at New York University, and earned a Silver Key in Critical Essay Writing from the Scholastic Art + Writing Awards.

In the future, I'd love to travel more. Whether it's flying to a different country or just taking a new route home, I love traveling and exploring. Having access to so many interesting opportunities that can inform my writing is one of the many perks of living in NYC and I can't wait for my range to broaden once I'm older and able to travel even more independently.
